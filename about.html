About

Ciara Kamahele

Instructor


I've been stoked on paragliding ever since I began flying in 2018 at the Point of the Mountain in Utah. I have a background in electrical and software engineering and love most outdoor adventure sports, especially climbing and whitewater rafting. Paragliding is such a unique sport - flying is magical, and learning about the atmosphere changed the way I appreciate the world on a daily basis. It is my sincere wish to share the joy and wonder I have gained from paragliding with you!
