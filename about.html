What is Hoon?

Hoon (noun): an Australian term describing a person who deliberately drives a vehicle in a reckless or dangerous manner.
Hoon activities (or hooning) can include drifting, burnouts, donuts, or screeching tires.[1]

At Hoon Fabrication, we take pride in making every car a hoon car. In short:

We'll make it hoon!
(Please hoon responsibly!)

Who is hooning at Hoon Fabrication?

Getting the project car going is what Shaun has been doing for years. He's been hooning every car he's had regardless of the car's ability to do so. Some of his fondest memories are perfecting his e-brake turns on icy roads in his grandma's station wagon. Rest assured that a percentage of every dollar spent at Hoon Fabrication will go back into one of his project cars, making each of them as hoonable as possible.

Earning a degreee in electronics led Shaun into a career of high-end manufacturing. He has built cameras that have traveled to the bottom of the ocean, deep space, and to the surface of Mars. In search of work that was more aligned with his automotive passions, he worked as a technician at Agile Off-Road in San Diego, CA and then opened Hoon Fabrication in Salt Lake City, UT. He has a deep understanding of what it takes to make quality products and a dedication to delighting his customers.
