Suspension installation and tuning

Vehicle suspension may be the most important part of your vehicles performance. We have experience installing and setting up aftermarket and factory suspension. Coil overs, leaf springs, struts and shocks can all make a huge differnce in your vehicle's dynamics.

Agile Offroad RIP kit installation. I have installed Agile RIP kits on over 50 vehicles. No need to make a big trip for an install that can be done at your home or our rented comercial space.

Sway bar installation and upgrades. Often overlooked and misunderstood is your vehicle's sway bar. Upgrading or adding a sway bar can make a world of differnce through the turns.

We also offer suspension evaluations, vehicle weighing, and corner balancing. Give us a call or text and we can get you set up.

Solar and auxilliary power installation

Solar pannels can give you the luxuries of the home while living off the grid. We have designed and installed solar on many differnt platforms. We can outfit solar on your vehicle that fits your exact needs.

If a complete solar installation is too complicated or overkill an auxilliary battery system charged by your vehicle's alternator may be the cheapest and simplest solution. When done correctly this system can work as a back-up to your vehicle's main battery and can even be used to jumpstart your vehicle. There are many options for an auxilliary system and considerations must be made about desired capacity and your stock alternator's ability to charge your system. We also offer high output and additional alternator installation.
