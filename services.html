Hoon Fabrication Services

Suspension Installation and Tuning

Vehicle suspension is essential to your vehicle's performance. We have experience installing and tuning aftermarket and factory suspension systems. Coilovers, leaf springs, struts, and shocks all make a huge difference in your vehicle's dynamics.

Agile Off-Road RIP kit installation: We have installed Agile RIP kits on over 50 vehicles. No need to make a big trip for an install that can be done close to home.

Sway bar installation and upgrades: Often overlooked and misunderstood is your vehicle's sway bar. Upgrading or adding a sway bar can make a world of difference through the turns.

We also offer suspension evaluations, vehicle weighing, and corner balancing.

Accessories Installation


We can install any aftermarket accessory such as: light bars, aftermarket bumpers, Agile Off-Road "no rub" front fenders, winches, skid plates, ARB on-board air, replacement seats, pedal boxes, aftermarket horns, tire racks, ladders, water tanks, WeBoost, Starlink, and more!

Fuel Tank Upgrades


Upgrade your Sprinter's fuel system and save trips to the gas station, relieve anxiety about making it to your next destination, and ensure your heater will make it through the night. We have experience installing Agile Off-Road, ACGB, S&B, and Owl Vans fuel tanks.

Solar and Auxiliary Power Installation


Solar panels give you the luxury of home while living in the wild. We have designed and installed solar on many differnt platforms. We can outfit solar on your vehicle to fit your exact needs.

If solar installation is overkill, an auxiliary battery system charged by your vehicle's alternator is a simple and cost-effective solution. Auxiliary batteries can expand yor power capacity and even jump-start your vehicle when nobody else is around. We also offer additonal or high-output alternator installation for people who have a serious demand for power.

Toyota Tercel 4WD Wagon Restoration and Repair


We have been collecting, daily-driving and restoring Toyota Tercel 4WD wagons for the last 10 years. Our passion is keeping these unique and highly capable vehicles out of scrap yards. We would love to help you with any issues or work you need done on your beloved Tercel or other vintage vehicle.

CAD Drafting and 3D Modeling


Our experience with computer-aided design in SolidWorks can bring your idea to life. Custom brakets, racks, railings, crossmembers, and roll cages are all within our range of capabilities. We can fabricate in-house or provide models for 3D printing and 2D drawings to send anywhere. Contact us and let's get started on your custom project.
